Research

Smart Phone Application for Fault Recognition

Guide: Dr. Nishchal K. Verma
R&D Project, IIT Kanpur (Summer 2012)

We developed a Smart Phone application, to learn different fault states of an industrial air compressor. The application was tested to recognize the fault state in real time as the air compressor was running. It has performed very well with classification accuracies above 93.73%. It is believed that similar application and model with some minor changes in specifications can be used for acoustic pattern recognition in wide range of fields; even in industry.

The paper Smart Phone Application for Fault Recognition has been accepted for ICST 2012
